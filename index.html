📌 What Are Gender Laws?
🚫

Prevent discrimination

based on sex, gender, or sexual orientation — ensuring fair treatment in all spaces.

🛡️

Protect from violence

shield individuals from gender-based violence, harassment, and abuse.

⚖️

Promote equality

equal opportunities, resources, and access to justice for all genders.

📚 11+ Laws for Equality & Protection
RA 9710

Magna Carta of Women

Comprehensive bill of rights for women — protects against discrimination in all areas of life.

RA 6725

Anti-Discrimination in Employment

Bans hiring, promotion, or training bias based on sex.

RA 7192

Women in Development Act

Integrates women in development, equal access to resources & opportunities.

RA 7877

Anti-Sexual Harassment Act

Unlawful sexual harassment in work, education, or training environments.

RA 7600

Breastfeeding Act

Requires workplaces to support breastfeeding & lactation stations.

RA 7322

Expanded Maternity Leave

Enhanced maternity benefits for female workers (right, not charity).

RA 9262

Anti-VAWC Act

Protects women & children from physical, sexual, psychological & economic abuse by intimate partners.

RA 8353

Anti-Rape Law

Rape as crime against persons, includes marital rape — vital shift.

RA 9208

Anti-Trafficking in Persons Act

Prohibits trafficking, especially of women & children; penalizes exploiters.

RA 7688

Women in Military Act

Allows women to hold combat roles in the military — equal service.

RA 10354

Reproductive Health Law

Access to family planning, sex education, and maternal care for all.

⚖️ SOGIE Bill (proposed)

SOGIE Equality Act

Would prohibit discrimination based on Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, Expression — extending protections to LGBTQIA+.

💡 Real-Life Application of Laws — with visual stories
RA 9262 (VAWC)

Economic abuse at home

Scenario: Wife experiences financial control — husband denies money for food.
Law works: She can file a Protection Order; barangay/court mandates financial support & protection.

RA 7877 (Sexual Harassment)

Unwanted jokes at work

Scenario: Male employee receives sexual jokes from supervisor.
Law works: Company investigates; supervisor faces admin & criminal liability.

RA 10354 (RH Law)

Teen denied contraception

Scenario: Clinic refuses due to personal beliefs.
Law works: Clinic must refer to another provider; DOH can sanction violations.

RA 6725 (Employment)

Gender bias in hiring

Scenario: Qualified female rejected for managerial role due to "men are better leaders".
Law works: DOLE complaint filed; employer fined for discrimination.

🔍 Critical Reflection — Progress & Gaps

✅ How laws promote equality

  • Recognize women’s rights as human rights
  • Criminalize violence & harassment
  • Access to health, employment & safety nets
  • Shift from “charity” to “entitlement” (maternity leave as right)
  • Proposed SOGIE Bill extends protections to LGBTQIA+

⚠️ Remaining Issues

  • Poor implementation & enforcement
  • Lack of awareness among citizens & authorities
  • Cultural/religious resistance (RH law, SOGIE Bill)
  • Underreporting of VAWC, rape, harassment due to stigma
  • No national SOGIE law yet — LGBTQIA+ remain vulnerable
  • Gaps in access for rural, indigenous, disabled women
🏛️ Why Gender Laws Matter Today

Gender-based violence and discrimination persist — laws set minimum standards and accountability.
Are these laws enough? No — we need the SOGIE Bill, stronger enforcement, budget allocation, and public education.
